About Us

Totally Reptiles is a medium-sized family-owned business located in Greensborough & now Kangaroo Flat.

Specialising in a wide range of Reptiles, Birds & Fish such as lizards, snakes, frogs, geckos, turtles, birds (ranging from small parrots to large), fish (from goldfish through to Australian native fish), spiders & scorpions including all the needs and accessories for these animals.

We pride ourselves in delivering quality and 100% customer service as a family business.
